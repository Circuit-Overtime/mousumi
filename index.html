    Mousumi Bhattacharya is an active performer and choreographer with over 30 years of experience in Bharatnatyam, ballet, and related disciplines. A member of the Tanushree Shankar Dance Company alongside Anita Mallick (Saraswat), she has performed extensively in ballet and aerobics. Certified by the esteemed Ananda Shankar, she trained in Kathak under Pt. Omprakash Maharaj and learned folk dance from Guru Botu Pal. She has participated in and organized numerous dance, ballet, aerobics, and fitness workshops. As the founder of Mayuri Centre for Aerobic and Rhythmic Arts, she has contributed to the field with remarkable dedication and stability.
